    • Welcome to Nagie's Angels Educational Center


    Nagie’s Angels is home to over 1000 learners from ages 1 to 14 years old, and more than 600 past students, who are characterized by their positive attitude towards life, godliness, intelligence and diligence. With over 30 years’ experience in providing quality teaching and child-centered learning practices for our learners, affectionately called “angels”, our school has given many children the opportunity to explore various study scopes, discover modern technologies in education, unearth hidden talents and connect with other children from across the 16 regions of Ghana and parts of West Africa.


    We make sure that none of our learners is left behind academically, by the relentless efforts of highly qualified and dedicated facilitators who give undivided attention to even the slowest of them. This has helped to make our angels confident and hardworking in their quest to attain excellent results in all their assessments.


    Our facilities are ultra-modern, including spacious and well-ventilated classrooms, fully equipped computer laboratory and library, an inviting art and home economics room. We also have numerous safe places for our angels to play and exercise, especially on our football pitch and basketball/volleyball court.


    Knowing that a healthy mind is found in a healthy body, our canteen provides nutritious and hot meals for lunch and freshly baked snacks and juices are sold throughout the day. In addition, we run free shuttle services from our Pre-school to our Basic school campuses every morning and afternoon.
