    • our vision & our mission

    our vision


    To be the centre of academic and moral excellence.
    At Nagie's Angels Educational Center we have a deep and unequivocal commitment to provide an education that is unsurpassed anywhere in the country.
    Our educational curriculum is based on the GES outline, but we are not limitied this curriculum. We also engage in extra curricular activities that are known to boost your child's brain and keep them healthy.


    our mission


    To provide and maintain excellent teaching and holistic training for all our pupils; inculcating in them character and competence, towards good and responsible leadership in Ghana and the world at large.
